Digital Transformation

Digital transformation is the process of using digital technologies to fundamentally change how organizations operate, deliver services, and create value.

In the public sector, like in government ministries, it means improving services, efficiency, and transparency by adopting tools such as online platforms, data systems, automation, and digital communication.

It's not just about technology—it's about changing mindsets, processes, and structures to better serve people in a digital world.
